About Nepali Unicode

Nepali Unicode is the standard digital encoding system for Nepali language text that works across all platforms and devices. Unlike traditional Nepali fonts that require special installation, Unicode Nepali works everywhere - from social media to email clients to websites.

Why Use Nepali Unicode?

  • Universal Compatibility: Works on all devices, browsers, and operating systems without needing special fonts
  • Easy to Use: Simply type in Romanized Nepali (like "namaste") and our tool converts it to proper Unicode
  • Social Media Ready: Perfect for Facebook posts, Twitter tweets, Instagram captions, and WhatsApp messages
  • Professional Use: Create documents, emails, and presentations in proper Nepali script
  • No Installation Needed: Works directly in your web browser with no downloads required

How to Use This Nepali Typing Tool

Our converter makes typing in Nepali simple:

  1. Type Nepali words using English letters in the box above (e.g., "mero naam Ram ho")
  2. The tool automatically converts your text to Nepali Unicode characters
  3. Copy the converted text and paste it anywhere - Facebook, Word documents, emails, etc.

This Nepali Unicode converter is optimized for all modern browsers and devices. Whether you're using a computer, tablet, or smartphone, you can easily type in Nepali wherever you need it.

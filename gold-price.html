About Gold Prices in Nepal

The gold price in Nepal fluctuates daily based on international market rates and local demand. Prices are typically updated multiple times per day by the Nepal Gold and Silver Dealers Association.

Key Factors Affecting Gold Prices:

  • International gold market prices (USD per ounce)
  • USD to NPR exchange rate
  • Local demand and market conditions
  • Government taxes and regulations

Common Gold Measurements in Nepal:

  • Tola: 11.6638 grams
  • Pau: ½ Tola (5.8319 grams)
  • Dharni: 2.5 Tola (29.1595 grams)

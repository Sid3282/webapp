Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a bug or issue?

You can report any bugs or issues by sending us a message through the contact form or emailing us directly at support@hamroutility.com. Please include as much detail as possible about the issue.

How often are gold prices updated?

Our gold prices are updated twice daily, in the morning and evening, based on the latest rates from the Nepal Gold and Silver Dealers' Association (NEGOSIDA).

Can I suggest a new tool or feature?

Absolutely! We welcome all suggestions for new tools or features. Please use the contact form to share your ideas with us.

Is Hamro Utility available in Nepali language?

Currently, we're working on Nepali language support which will be available in our next update. Stay tuned!

About Hamro Utility

Hamro Utility was created by Siddh Batala to provide essential daily tools for Nepali people in one convenient, mobile-friendly platform. Our goal is to make everyday tasks easier, whether you're checking dates, gold prices, or weather forecasts.

All our tools are designed with simplicity and speed in mind, ensuring they work well even on low-bandwidth connections common in many parts of Nepal.

Our Features

  • Accurate date conversion between Bikram Sambat and Gregorian calendars
  • Real-time gold and silver prices updated throughout the day
  • Currency exchange rates from reliable sources
  • Weather forecasts for major Nepali cities
  • Astrological information and panchang
  • All tools optimized for mobile devices
  • Low bandwidth consumption

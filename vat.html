VAT Tips for Nepal

General VAT Information

  • Standard VAT rate in Nepal is 13% (as of 2024)
  • Businesses with turnover over Rs. 2 million annually must register for VAT
  • Always mention VAT registration number on invoices

VAT Exemptions in Nepal

  • Basic food items (rice, lentils, vegetables)
  • Educational books and materials
  • Medicines and medical equipment
  • Agricultural inputs (fertilizers, seeds)

For Businesses

  • VAT returns must be filed monthly by the 25th of following month
  • Maintain proper VAT invoices for all transactions
  • Keep records for at least 6 years as per IRD requirements

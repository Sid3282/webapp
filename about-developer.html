Siddha Batala

Creator of Hamro Utility

Hi, I'm Siddha Batala, the developer behind Hamro Utility. Originally from Lamki, Kailali and currently based in Kathmandu, I come from a non-developer background but have a strong passion for coding, technology, and learning new things every day.

My journey into programming began as a hobby, but it quickly turned into a passion as I discovered the power of technology to solve everyday problems. Hamro Utility was born out of my desire to create useful tools that make life easier for Nepali people.

As a self-taught developer, I believe in continuous learning and improvement. Every day presents an opportunity to grow, and I'm excited to keep building tools that serve our community.

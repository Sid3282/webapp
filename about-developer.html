Creator of Hamro Utility

Hello! I'm Siddha Batala, the developer behind Hamro Utility. Originally from Lamki, Kailali and now based in Kathmandu, I created this platform to provide essential daily tools for Nepali people in one convenient place.

Coming from a non-technical background, I taught myself coding to build solutions for everyday problems. Hamro Utility is my way of contributing to our community by making useful tools accessible to everyone.

My goal is to keep improving Hamro Utility with more features while maintaining its simplicity and speed, especially for users with limited internet access.

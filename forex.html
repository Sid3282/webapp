About Forex Rates in Nepal

The foreign exchange rates in Nepal are regulated by Nepal Rastra Bank (NRB). Commercial banks and exchange counters must follow the rates set by NRB, with a small margin allowed for their services.

Key Points About Forex in Nepal:

  • Exchange rates are updated daily by Nepal Rastra Bank
  • Banks and exchange counters may have slightly different rates
  • USD is the most traded foreign currency in Nepal
  • There are limits on how much foreign currency you can buy/sell without documentation

Common Exchange Rates:

  • USD to NPR: Most commonly used rate for remittance and trade
  • EUR to NPR: Important for European travelers and trade
  • GBP to NPR: Relevant for UK-Nepal remittance and travel
  • INR to NPR: Fixed at 1.6 NPR per INR (as per Nepal-India currency agreement)

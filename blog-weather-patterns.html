🗺️ The Geography Behind the Weather

Nepal’s diverse terrain—from tropical lowlands to arctic highlands—creates five major climate zones that influence everything from agriculture to daily clothing choices.

🌦️ Seasonal Variations

  • Spring (Mar–May): Warm and blooming.
  • Monsoon (Jun–Aug): Heavy rainfall and humidity.
  • Autumn (Sep–Nov): Clear skies, ideal for travel.
  • Winter (Dec–Feb): Cold, especially in the hills and mountains.

📍 Regional Highlights

Terai: Hot summers, foggy winters.
Hills (e.g., Kathmandu): Moderate, but monsoon-sensitive.
Mountains: Cold year-round, with snow in winter.

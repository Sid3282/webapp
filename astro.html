Understanding Nepali Astrology

What is Panchanga?

Panchanga is the traditional Hindu astrological calendar that provides five essential elements of time:

  • Tithi: Lunar day
  • Nakshatra: Constellation
  • Yoga: Auspicious/inauspicious periods
  • Karana: Half of a tithi
  • Vaar: Day of the week

About Rashifal (Horoscope)

Nepali Rashifal is based on Vedic astrology and provides daily predictions for all 12 zodiac signs:

Mesh (Aries)
Brish (Taurus)
Mithun (Gemini)
Karkat (Cancer)
Singha (Leo)
Kanya (Virgo)
Tula (Libra)
Brischik (Scorpio)
Dhanu (Sagittarius)
Makar (Capricorn)
Kumbha (Aquarius)
Meen (Pisces)

How to Use This Information

  1. Check today's Panchanga for auspicious timings (shubha muhurta)
  2. Read your Rashifal for daily guidance
  3. Plan important activities according to favorable planetary positions
  4. Note inauspicious periods (like Rahu Kaal) to avoid
  5. Use this information as guidance rather than absolute prediction

Cultural Significance

Astrology plays an important role in Nepali culture for:

  • Determining wedding dates and other ceremonies
  • Naming newborns according to nakshatra
  • Starting new ventures and business activities
  • Planning travel and important life events
  • Understanding personality traits and compatibilities

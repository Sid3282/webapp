About Unit Conversion

Measurement Systems

There are two main systems of measurement used worldwide:

  • Metric System: Used by most countries, based on units like meters, grams, and liters
  • Imperial System: Primarily used in the United States, based on units like feet, pounds, and gallons

Common Conversion Factors

Type Metric Unit Imperial Unit Conversion
Length 1 Centimeter Inch 1 cm = 0.3937 in
Weight 1 Kilogram Pound 1 kg = 2.2046 lbs
Area 1 Square Meter Square Foot 1 m² = 10.764 ft²
Volume 1 Liter Gallon (US) 1 L = 0.2642 gal
Temperature °Celsius °Fahrenheit °C × 9/5 + 32 = °F

Practical Usage Tips

  1. For construction projects, convert between feet and meters accurately
  2. When cooking, convert between grams and ounces for recipes
  3. For land measurement, convert between hectares and acres
  4. When traveling, convert between kilometers and miles
  5. For weather, convert between Celsius and Fahrenheit

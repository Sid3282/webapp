Current Weather Patterns

Seasonal Weather Guide

  • Spring (March-May): Mild temperatures, occasional rain, ideal for trekking
  • Summer/Monsoon (June-August): Heavy rainfall, high humidity, warm temperatures
  • Autumn (September-November): Clear skies, moderate temperatures, peak tourist season
  • Winter (December-February): Cold in mountains, mild in valleys, dry weather

Weather Safety Tips

  1. Check forecasts regularly during monsoon for flood alerts
  2. Prepare for temperature drops in mountainous regions
  3. Carry rain gear during summer months
  4. Monitor air quality in Kathmandu Valley during dry seasons
  5. Check road conditions before travel during heavy rains

Understanding Weather Data

Our weather widget displays:

  • Temperature: Current air temperature in Celsius
  • Humidity: Relative humidity percentage
  • Rainfall: Precipitation measurements
  • Wind: Speed and direction
  • Pressure: Atmospheric pressure readings
